How do I update my response time settings?

You can update your typical response time in your Account Settings under the "Account Setting" section. Choose between "Instantly", "Within 3 to 5 hours", or "A little longer" to set buyer expectations.

What's the best way to connect my WhatsApp or Telegram?

In your Account Settings, paste your full WhatsApp or Telegram link (e.g., https://wa.me/1234567890) in the respective fields. This allows buyers to message you directly through these platforms.

How can I improve my visibility to buyers?
