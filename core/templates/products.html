Important: Bulk Upload Instructions

Quick Note: Required fields: name, strength, price, stock quantity, dosage form.

1. File Structure

The first row of your Excel must contain headers. Common accepted names:

Field Accepted Header Names Of Your Excel
Product Namename, product name, product_name
Strengthstrength, dose
Priceprice, cost, unit price
Stock Quantitystock_quantity, stock quantity, quantity, stock, stock qty
Dosage Formdosage_form_id, dosage, dosage form, form
Expiration Date (optional)expire_date, expiry, expire date, expiry date, expiration date

2. Supported Dosage Forms

Only the following dosage forms are accepted (case-insensitive):

  • Tablet, Capsule, Injection, Syrup, Suspension
  • Cream, Ointment, Gel, Lotion, Suppository
  • Powder, Drops (Eye/Ear/Nasal), Inhaler, Patch, Granules
  • Mouthwash, Lozenge, Solution, Emulsion, Spray

3. Data Rules

  • Required fields: name, strength, price, stock_quantity, dosage_form_id.
  • Dosage Form must match exactly one of the supported forms.
  • Expiration Date is optional; if present, it must be a valid date (YYYY-MM-DD, MM/DD/YYYY).

4. Upload Behavior

  • Existing products (same name, strength, dosage form, supplier) are updated.
  • New products are created if no match exists.
  • Errors are recorded per row; upload continues even if some rows fail.

5. Post-Upload Feedback

  • Success: shows how many new products were created and how many existing products were updated.
  • Errors: lists row numbers with problems for easy correction.
