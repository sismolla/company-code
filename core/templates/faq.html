Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ordering pharmaceutical products through our platform.

Ordering Process

How do I place an order with a supplier?

To place an order, follow these simple steps:

  1. Browse the supplier's product catalog.
  2. Select the products and quantities you need.
  3. Go to the supplier page and click the add button to order.
  4. Review your order summary.
  5. Upload any required prescription documents.
  6. Submit your order request.

The supplier will review your order and contact you to confirm the details and arrange for payment.

Can I modify or cancel my order after submission?

Order modifications or cancellations must be requested directly with the supplier. Please contact them immediately if you need to make changes. Once an order has been sent, it will be available for 7 consecutive days in the system. After this period, orders are no longer active.

Most suppliers can accommodate changes if requested before order processing begins.

How do I know if a product is in stock?

Each product listing displays its current stock status:

  • Stock Quantity: The available quantity in stock.
  • Out of Stock: The product is currently unavailable or has been reserved by a customer on a first-come, first-served basis.

For critical medications, we recommend contacting the supplier directly to confirm availability before placing your order.

Prescriptions

Do I need to upload a prescription for every order?

Prescriptions are required only for products classified as prescription medications. Our system will prompt you to upload the necessary document during the checkout process. This ensures compliance with all pharmaceutical regulations.

What is a valid prescription?

A valid prescription must be issued by a licensed medical professional and include the following:

  • Patient's full name and address.
  • Prescriber's full name, address, and license number.
  • Date of issue.
  • Name, strength, and quantity of the medication.
  • Dosage Form.

Payments

What payment methods are accepted?

Payments are handled directly between the supplier and the customer. PharmacyConnect does not process or manage any payment gateways. We strongly recommend using secure and common payment methods.

  • Avoid unconventional payment methods.
  • Always confirm payment details directly with the supplier before transferring funds.
  • We do not recommend making payments based solely on trust without verified contact information.

Is my payment information secure?

Since all payments are handled directly with the supplier, your payment security depends on the method and platform you use. We advise you to use established and reputable payment services and to verify the supplier's payment instructions carefully. PharmacyConnect does not store or have access to any of your financial information.

Delivery

What are the delivery options and timeframes?

Delivery options and timeframes are managed by the individual suppliers and you should contact them directly to confirm details. However, typical options may include:

Service Timeframe
Standard 3-5 business days
Express 1-2 business days
Same-day Within 24 hours

Actual delivery times may vary based on product availability and your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been confirmed and shipped by the supplier, they will provide you with available information.

Account

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Join" button on the top right corner of the homepage. You will need to provide your business information and complete a verification process to ensure you are a licensed healthcare professional or a pharmacy business. This process helps us maintain a secure and professional community.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login page, click on "Forgot Password?". Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you an email with instructions on how to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam or junk folder.
